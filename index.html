Our Story

From Vision
to Reality

Everwood Interiors was born from a carpenter's dream — the belief that spaces hold power to transform the people who inhabit them.

Based in Areekode, Malappuram, we've grown from a two-person team into Malabar's most trusted design studio, blending traditional craftsmanship with forward-looking aesthetics.

We don't just design interiors. We listen to your life, understand your aspirations, and build spaces that feel like they were always meant to be yours.
